I2E LAB: Innovation to Entrepreneurship LAB

Together with Telengana Innovation Cell, India’s leading innovation and entrepreneurship ecosystem, we, The Make Room India, bring you the i2E Lab: Innovation to Entrepreneurship Lab, a three month virtual Pre-Incubation Program.

i2E Lab is designed to enable youth entrepreneurship, with a special focus on students and recent graduates to kick-start their entrepreneurship journey. i2E Lab will facilitate as a platform to experiment with ideas, learn by doing and turn those ideas into viable products.

In the next three months, you will get your hands dirty, delve deeper into the problems you want to solve, acquire new skills, and take your Idea to Prototype / Proof of Concept (PoC) level.

i2E LAB Program Structure:

1)Workshops from industry experts on TWO days a week Each module spread over two weeks

2)One-on-One Mentoring - every weekend, pre-scheduled with each team

What do we offer to participants?

Demo Day: Pitch for Prototyping / PoC Grants & further Incubation support – up to INR 12 Lakh for winning teams;

Access to a vast network from across Asia & Europe;

Support from Telangana State Innovation Cell;

Cloud Credits, Innovation Kit and other benefits worth INR 1.6 Lakh per startup;Validate your product/innovation-market fit;

A chance to pitch your company to angel investors and incubators;

Learn from national & international experts– entrepreneurs, innovators and industry professionals;

Access to partner prototyping labs across India.

Participating teams will pitch to secure Prototyping /PoC Grants of up to INR 12 Lakh & Incubation support

Programme fees

To build and deliver this program, we have invested of over INR 40000 per startup. We believe in enabling youth to build for the nation, bring all the resources, connections and talent together to re-imagine possibilities. Keeping this in mind, we have subsidized this program to be as affordable as possible.

Upon selection, you will have to pay a non-refundable commitment fees of INR 6500, per team.  In return, each team will receive benefits worth INR 1.6 Lakh  in program content, startup cloud credits, access to innovation tools, mentorship, prototyping labs, memberships and other resources.

What kind of ideas are a match?

We are keen to work with solutions that will solve & build for India – right from education to access to health care; from agricultural innovations to retail solutions; from reducing digital divide & income inequalities to creating new livelihood opportunities; from mental health care to mobile apps enabling local consumption – we welcome all!

Sector agnostic and invite ideas to solve for SDGs & Build4India; Most suited for early stage ideas, with or without a proof of concept; Both commercial and social business models are welcome. Tech / non-tech solutions;
Both commercial and social business models are welcome.

The program is designed to help you fine tune your ideas – move up from abstract level to build prototypes and minimum viable product.
